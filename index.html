About Our Academy

Rooted in Kumbotso, focused on every student's future

Path to Success International Academy is a secondary school located in Wailari, opposite Sa'adatu Rimi College, Kumbotso LGA, Kano State. Since opening in 2022, the academy has been committed to delivering quality, values-driven secondary education to students in the community and beyond.

We believe education is more than passing examinations — it is about shaping disciplined, confident young people ready to lead in their communities and careers.

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable secondary education that equips students with knowledge, skills and strong moral character.

Our Vision

To be a leading secondary school in Kano State, known for academic excellence and disciplined graduates.
